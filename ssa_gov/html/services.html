Our Services:

San Andreas Social Security Administration Services: The Social Secuirty Administration of San Andreas has a few Services that help the people of San Andreas live with disablities or temporary support from their government. Our services include but are not limited to: 1) SSI (Supplemental Security Income) 2) SSDI (Social Security Disability Income) To apply for either you need to be able to prove that you are in fact disabled and you can do so in the following ways: 1) Notes from a Therapist 2) Records from a Doctor 3) Evidence on video or by picture
